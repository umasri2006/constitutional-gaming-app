How to Play

Level 1: Article Ninja

Tap on the articles as they appear on screen to learn about them.

You need to score 30/39 points to advance to Level 2.

Level 2: Match the Articles

Match article numbers to their correct explanations.

10 matches to complete with 15 options.

More than 5 mistakes will send you back to Level 1.

Scoring

  • Start with 100 coins
  • Each correct match: +1 coin
  • All 10 matches correct: +20 bonus coins
  • Help or replay: -40 coins
